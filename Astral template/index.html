Why a dissertation doula?

If you're getting ready to hand in your dissertation, this is probably the most stressed you've ever been in your life. I know because I've supported a number of friends and clients through the last stages.

I offer empathetic support and encouragement throughout my services helping you prepare for submission. You can do this! You're nearly there!

Editing for non-native speakers

You might be writing your dissertation in English even though that is not your first language.

I can make sure it reads well for native speakers. My comments might also help you be more precise in the way you express your thinking.

Proofreading specifically for dissertations

Even if you're a native English-speaker, you probably can't bear to look at those chapters anymore.

I can provide you with proofreading, checking not only the language but also make sure you reference all of your figures and tables in the text and that all your references appear in your bibliography.

Formatting according to your university's style guide

To take some of the pressure off, delegate the final formatting to me. I will follow your university's style guide to a T.

Even if you are not concerned about language, you might not have the energy or headspace to do that final formatting: checking margins, making sure the table of contents adequately reflects the headers your thesis, checking that the figures and/or tables are properly numbered and all appear in the table of figures and/or tables... I can even format your bibliography!

Proofreading
specifically for dissertations

Even if you're a native English speaker, you probably can't bear to look at those chapters anymore.

I can provide you with thorough proofreading not just of the text, but of your dissertation as a whole, from its title page to the last appendix.

  • Do you have any spelling or grammar mistakes left?
  • Are your headers consistent (verbs or nouns, with or without articles like "the" and "a")?
  • Do all your figures and tables, including rows and columns, have a clear title?
  • Do you reference all your figures, tables, and appendices in the text?
  • Do all your references appear in your bibliography?
  • Get in touch

Formatting
according to your university's style guide

To take some of the pressure off, delegate the final formatting to me. I will accurately follow your university's style guide.

  • Margins and title page
  • Font size of headers
  • Table of contents, of tables, and/or of figures
  • Numbering of headers, tables, figures, and/or appendices
  • Widows and orphans (lone lines at the beginning or end of a page)
  • Positioning of tables and/or figures, with their titles, on the page
  • Bibliography or works cited
  • Get in touch
